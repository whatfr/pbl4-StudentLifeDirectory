SLD

This website is designed as a smart parking solution tailored for modern urban environments. It provides users with real-time information on available parking spaces, allows them to register for parking, and offers discounts to registered members. Our system integrates various features to enhance the user experience, ensuring a seamless parking experience whether users are at home or on the go.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in the required information. After submitting, you’ll receive a confirmation notification box. Then, you can log in and access your account.

Are the discounts only for students?

Yes, the discounts listed on our site are exclusively for students. You may need to show a valid student ID at participating businesses to redeem discounts.

What should I do if a discount isn’t available or has expired?

We strive to keep information up-to-date, but occasionally discounts may change without notice. If you find an expired discount, please let us know through the Contact Us page.

Do I need to pay to use the site?

No, our website is free to use! We aim to help students find affordable options without any added costs.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click on “Forgot Password,” enter your registered email, and follow the instructions to reset your password.
